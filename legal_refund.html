Refund Policy

When Refunds Are Considered

Refunds are considered if:

  • A job cannot be completed as agreed (runner cancels, emergency, etc.)
  • A customer cancels before a runner starts
  • A significant error is made that warrants refund

When Refunds Are NOT Provided

Refunds are NOT provided for:

  • Change of mind by customer (job already in progress)
  • Customer-requested cancellations after payment confirmed
  • Disputes over job quality (unless job was not done)
  • External circumstances beyond RUNNER's control

How to Request a Refund

Contact RUNNER directly with your Job ID and reason for refund. We will review within 5 business days. If approved, refunds are issued by the original payment method (or cash).

Refund Process

EFT refunds may take 3-5 business days to appear in your account. Cash refunds are issued immediately when possible. PayShap and QR refunds follow PayShap's standard process.

Disputes

If you disagree with our refund decision, you may dispute within 14 days. We will make a final decision in good faith.
